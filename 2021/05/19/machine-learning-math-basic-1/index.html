高斯分布

概率密度

假设$p$为研究的维度，$\mu$为均值向量，

$X \sim N(\mu, \Sigma)=\dfrac{1}{(2\pi)^{\frac{p}{2}}|\Sigma|^{\frac{1}{2}}}exp^{-\frac{1}{2}(x-\mu)^T\Sigma^{-1}(x-\mu)}$

当$p=1$时，$p(X=x)=\dfrac{e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\Sigma}}}{\sqrt{2\pi\Sigma}}$

首先求最大似然估计(MLE)，$\theta_{MLE}=\underset{\theta=\{\mu, \Sigma\}}{\mathrm{argmax}} \prod\limits_{i=1}^n p(X=x_i)=\underset{\theta=\{\mu, \Sigma\}}{\mathrm{argmax}}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n({-\frac{(x_i-\mu)^2}{2\Sigma}})-nlog(\sqrt{2\pi\Sigma})$

先求$\mu_{MLE}$，$f(\mu)=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (x_i-\mu)^2$

$f’(\mu)=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n -2(x_i-\mu)=2\mu n-2\sum\limits_{i=1}^nx_i$

令$f’(\mu)=0$，则$\mu=\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^nx_i=\mu_{MLE}$

接着求$\Sigma_{MLE}$，$f(\Sigma)=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n({-\frac{(x_i-\mu)^2}{2\Sigma}})-nlog(\sqrt{2\pi\Sigma})$，

$f’(\Sigma)=\dfrac{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (x_i-\mu)^2}{2\Sigma^2}-\dfrac{n}{2\Sigma}=0$

$\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (x_i-\mu)^2=n\Sigma$

$\Sigma=\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (x_i-\mu_{MLE})^2=\Sigma_{MLE}$

计算$E(\mu_{MLE})=E(\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n x_i)=\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^nE(x_i)=\dfrac{1}{n} n \mu=\mu$

$\Sigma_{MLE}=\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n x_i^2+ \dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\mu_{MLE}^2-2\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n x_i\mu_{MLE}=\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n x_i^2+\mu^2_{MLE}-2\mu_{MLE}^2 = \dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (x_i^2 - \mu^2_{MLE})$
$$
\begin{align}
E(\Sigma_{MLE})&=E(\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (x_i^2 - \mu^2_{MLE}))\\\
&=E(\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n [x_i^2 - \mu^2 -( \mu^2_{MLE} - \mu^2)])\\\
&=(\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n E(x_i^2)-E^2(x_i)-E(\mu^2_{MLE})+E(\mu^2)))\\\
&=\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n D(x_i) + (E^2(\mu_{MLE})-E(\mu^2_{MLE})) \\\
&=\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n D(x_i) - D(\mu_{MLE}) \\\
&=\Sigma-D(\mu_{MLE})\\\
&=\Sigma - D(\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n x_i) \\\
&=\Sigma-\dfrac{1}{n^2}nD(x_i)\\\
&=\Sigma- \dfrac{1}{n}\Sigma \\\
&=\dfrac{n-1}{n}\Sigma
\end{align}
$$
发现$E(\mu_{MLE})=\mu, E(\Sigma_{MLE}) \neq \Sigma$，因此$\mu_{MLE}$作为$\mu$的估计是无偏的，$\Sigma_{MLE}$作为$\Sigma$的估计是有偏的。
