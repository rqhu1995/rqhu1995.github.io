高斯分布

概率密度

假设$p$为研究的维度，$\mu$为均值向量，$\Sigma$为协方差矩阵（$p\times p$）
$$
X \sim N(\mu, \Sigma)=\dfrac{1}{(2\pi)^{\frac{p}{2} }|\Sigma|^{\frac{1}{2} }}exp^{-\frac{1}{2}(x-\mu)^T\Sigma^{-1}(x-\mu)}
$$

一维的情况下的两个极大似然估计（Maximum Likelihood Estimation，MLE）

当$p=1$时，$p(X=x)=\dfrac{e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\Sigma} }}{\sqrt{2\pi\Sigma} }$
$$
\theta_{MLE}=\underset{\theta=\{\mu, \Sigma\} }{\mathrm{argmax} } \prod\limits_{i=1}^n p(X=x_i)=\underset{\theta=\{\mu, \Sigma\} }{\mathrm{argmax} }\sum\limits_{i=1}^n({-\frac{(x_i-\mu)^2}{2\Sigma} })-nlog(\sqrt{2\pi\Sigma})
$$

  • 先求$\mu_{MLE}$，
    $$
    f(\mu)=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (x_i-\mu)^2
    $$

$$
f’(\mu)=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n -2(x_i-\mu)=2\mu n-2\sum\limits_{i=1}^nx_i​
$$

令$f’(\mu)=0$，则

$$
\mu_{MLE}=\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^nx_i
$$

  • 接着求$\Sigma_{MLE}$，
    $$
    f(\Sigma)=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n({-\frac{(x_i-\mu)^2}{2\Sigma} })-nlog(\sqrt{2\pi\Sigma})
    $$

$$
f’(\Sigma)=\dfrac{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (x_i-\mu)^2}{2\Sigma^2}-\dfrac{n}{2\Sigma}$
$$

令$f’(\mu)=0$，则
$$
\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (x_i-\mu)^2=n\Sigma \
\Sigma_{MLE}=\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (x_i-\mu_{MLE})^2
$$

关于两个估计的无偏性

  • 计算$E(\mu_{MLE})$：
    $$
    E(\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n x_i)=\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^nE(x_i)=\dfrac{1}{n} n \mu=\mu
    $$

  • 计算$E(\Sigma_{MLE})$：

    • 首先对$\Sigma_{MLE}$做一个化简：

    $$
    \begin{align}
    \Sigma_{MLE} &=\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n x_i^2+ \dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\mu_{MLE}^2-2\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n x_i\mu_{MLE}\quad [note:\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n x_i=\mu_{MLE}]\\\
    &=\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n x_i^2+\mu^2_{MLE}-2\mu_{MLE}^2\\\
    &= \dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (x_i^2 - \mu^2_{MLE})
    \end{align}
    $$

  • 接着求其期望：

$$
\begin{align}
E(\Sigma_{MLE})&=E(\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (x_i^2 - \mu^2_{MLE}))\\\
&=E(\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n [x_i^2 - \mu^2 -( \mu^2_{MLE} - \mu^2)]) \quad –>恒等变形\\\
&=(\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n E(x_i^2)-E^2(x_i)-E(\mu^2_{MLE})+E(\mu^2)))\quad–>\mu^2看作常数，\mu^2=E(\mu^2)\\\
&=\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n D(x_i) + (E^2(\mu_{MLE})-E(\mu^2_{MLE}))\quad –>方差与概率间的公式转化\\\
&=\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n D(x_i) - D(\mu_{MLE})\quad –>方差与概率间的公式转化 \\\
&=\Sigma-D(\mu_{MLE})\\\
&=\Sigma - D(\dfrac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n x_i) \\\
&=\Sigma-\dfrac{1}{n^2}nD(x_i)\\\
&=\Sigma- \dfrac{1}{n}\Sigma \\\
&=\dfrac{n-1}{n}\Sigma
\end{align}
$$

  • 发现$E(\mu_{MLE})=\mu, E(\Sigma_{MLE}) \neq \Sigma$，因此$\mu_{MLE}$作为$\mu$的估计是无偏的，$\Sigma_{MLE}$作为$\Sigma$的估计是有偏的。

多维度的情况

观察指数部分，记为D：$(x-\mu)^T\Sigma^{-1}(x-\mu)$

$x$是一个样本向量：$x =\left(x_1,x_2,\ldots,x_n \right)^T$。

注意这部分的结果是一个标量（维度：（1,p)-(p,p)-(p,1)->(1,1)）

实际上，D是一个马氏距离，是向量$x$与向量$\mu$之间的马氏距离。

而$\Sigma=U \Lambda U^T=[u_1, u_2, \dots,u_p] \begin{bmatrix}
\lambda_{1} & & \
& \ddots & \
& & \lambda_{r}
\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}
u_{1}^T \
u_2^T \
\ldots \\ u_p^T
\end{bmatrix}=\sum\limits_{i=1}^{p}u_i\lambda_iu_i^T$

($U$与$U^T$互逆)

求逆：${(U^T)}^{-1}\Lambda^{-1}U^{-1}=\sum\limits_{i=1}^pu_i \dfrac{1}{\lambda_i}u_i^T$

马氏距离表示为：
$$
(x-\mu)^T \Sigma^{-1}(x-\mu)=
(x-\mu)^T \left(\sum\limits_{i=1}^pu_i \dfrac{1}{\lambda_i}u_i^T \right)(x-\mu)=
\sum\limits_{i=1}^{p}(x-\mu)^T u_i \dfrac{1}{\lambda_i}u_i^T(x-\mu)
$$

得到马氏距离为：$\sum\limits_{i=1}^{p}y_i^2/\lambda_i$

当维度为2时，可以看作是一个以$u_1$和$u_2$为直角坐标系下的椭圆（$y$的方向与$u$一致，因为$(x-\mu)^Tu_i$是在$u_i$上的投影）：

于是，高斯分布的概率可以理解为

马氏距离为n的时候，形成等概率线（椭圆上的点），如果把n算作第三维，实际上就是椭圆曲面的一个切面

高斯分布的局限性

$\Sigma$参数数量：对一个$p$维的高斯分布，参数数量过多

边缘概率分布$p$。$\def\vector#1.{\begin{pmatrix} #1 \end{pmatrix} }$

$X \sim (\mu, \Sigma)$

$$
X=\vector{x_1\\x_2\\\ldots\\x_p}.=\vector{x_a\\x_b}., \mu=\vector{\mu_a\\\mu_b}.,\Sigma=\begin{pmatrix}\Sigma_{aa} & \Sigma_{ab} \\ \Sigma_{ba} & \Sigma_{bb} \end{pmatrix}
$$
$x_a$，m维；$x_b$，n维，$m+n=p$

求$p(x_a), p(x_b \mid x_a)$

先给一个定理：$X \sim (\mu, \Sigma)$，$AX+B \sim (A\mu+B, A\Sigma A^T)$

$x_a =(I_m \ \ \ \ 0) \vector{x_a\\x_b}.$

$\therefore x_a \sim \left((I_m\ \ \ \ 0)\vector{\mu_a\\\mu_b}.=\mu_a, (I_m\ \ \ \ 0) \begin{pmatrix}\Sigma_{aa} & \Sigma_{ab} \\ \Sigma_{ba} & \Sigma_{bb} \end{pmatrix} \vector{I_m\\0}.=\Sigma_{aa}\right)$

Schur Complement:

$$
\begin{cases}
x_{b \cdot a}=x_b - \Sigma_{ba}\Sigma^{-1}_{aa}x_a \\\
\mu_{b \cdot a}=\mu_b - \Sigma_{ba}\Sigma^{-1}_{aa}\mu_a \\\
\Sigma_{bb \cdot a}=\Sigma_{bb} - \Sigma_{ba}\Sigma^{-1}_{aa}\Sigma_{ab}
\end{cases}
$$
矩阵表示：
$$
\begin{align}
x_{b \cdot a}&=\begin{pmatrix} -\Sigma_{ba}\Sigma^{-1}_{aa} \quad I \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} x_a \\ x_b \end{pmatrix}\\\
E[x_{b \cdot a}]&=E[\begin{pmatrix} -\Sigma_{ba}\Sigma^{-1}_{aa} \quad I \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} x_a \\ x_b \end{pmatrix}]=\begin{pmatrix} -\Sigma_{ba}\Sigma^{-1}_{aa} \quad I \end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix} \mu_a \\ \mu_b \end{pmatrix}=-\Sigma_{ba}\Sigma^{-1}_{aa}\mu_a+\mu_b=\mu_{b \cdot a}\\\
Var[x_{b \cdot a}]&=Var[\begin{pmatrix} -\Sigma_{ba}\Sigma^{-1}_{aa} \quad I \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} x_a \\ x_b \end{pmatrix}]=\begin{pmatrix} -\Sigma_{ba}\Sigma^{-1}_{aa} \quad I \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix}\Sigma_{aa} & \Sigma_{ab} \\ \Sigma_{ba} & \Sigma_{bb} \end{pmatrix} \vector{-(\Sigma_{aa}^{-1})^T\Sigma_{ba}^T \\ I}.
\\\\ & =(-\Sigma_{ba}\Sigma_{aa}^{-1}\Sigma_{aa}+\Sigma_{ba} \quad -\Sigma_{ba}\Sigma_{aa}^{-1}\Sigma_{ab}+\Sigma_{bb}) \vector{-(\Sigma_{aa}^{-1})^T\Sigma_{ba}^T \\ I}.\\\
&=(0 \quad -\Sigma_{ba}\Sigma_{aa}^{-1}\Sigma_{ab}+\Sigma_{bb}) \vector{-(\Sigma_{aa}^{-1})^T\Sigma_{ba}^T \\ I}.\\\
&=-\Sigma_{ba}\Sigma_{aa}^{-1}\Sigma_{ab}+\Sigma_{bb}=\Sigma_{bb \cdot a}
\end{align}
$$

$x_b=x_{b\cdot a}+\Sigma_{ba}\Sigma^{-1}_{aa}x_a$
$$
\begin{align}
E(x_b \mid x_a)&=E(x_{b \cdot a}+\Sigma_{ba}\Sigma^{-1}_{aa}x_a)=\mu_{b \cdot a} + \Sigma_{ba}\Sigma^{-1}_{aa}x_a\\\
Var(x_b \mid x_a)&=Var(x_{b\cdot a})=\Sigma_{bb \cdot a}
\end{align}
$$

$p(x)\sim N(x \mid \mu, \Lambda^{-1}), p(y \mid x) \sim N(y \mid Ax + b, L^{-1})$
$$
y=Ax+b+\varepsilon, \varepsilon \sim N(0, L^{-1})
$$
$E(y)=E(Ax+b+\varepsilon)=AE(x)+b+E(\varepsilon)=A\mu+b\\\\Var(y)=Var(Ax+b+\varepsilon)=A\Lambda^{-1}A^T+L^{-1}\\\\y \sim N(A\mu +b, A\Lambda^{-1}A^T+L^{-1})$

$z=\vector{x \\ y}. \sim N \left(\begin{bmatrix} \mu \\ A\mu +b \end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix} \Lambda^{-1} & \Delta \\ \Delta & A\Lambda^{-1}A^T+L^{-1} \end{bmatrix} \right)$

$\Delta=Cov(x, y) = E[(x-E(x))(y-E(y))^T]$
$$
\begin{align}
E[(x-E(x))(y-E(y))^T]&=E[(x-\mu)(Ax+b+\varepsilon-A\mu-b)^T]\\\
&=E[(x-\mu)(Ax-A\mu+\varepsilon)^T]\\\
&=E[(x-\mu)(x-\mu)^TA^T+(x-\mu)\varepsilon^T]\\\
&=Var(x)A^T+E[(x-\mu)\varepsilon^T]\\\
&=\Lambda^{-1}A^T + E(x-\mu)E(\varepsilon^T)\\\
&=\Lambda^{-1}A^T
\end{align}
$$
根据
$$
\begin{align}
E(x_b \mid x_a)&=E(x_{b \cdot a}+\Sigma_{ba}\Sigma^{-1}_{aa}x_a)=\mu_{b \cdot a} + \Sigma_{ba}\Sigma^{-1}_{aa}x_a\\\
Var(x_b \mid x_a)&=Var(x_{b\cdot a})=\Sigma_{bb \cdot a}
\end{align}
$$

$\therefore E(x \mid y) = \mu_{x\cdot y} + \Sigma_{xy}\Sigma^{-1}_{yy}y=\mu_x-\Sigma_{xy}\Sigma^{-1}_{yy}\mu_y+ \Sigma_{xy}\Sigma^{-1}_{yy}y= \mu-\Sigma_{xy}\Sigma^{-1}_{yy}(\mu_y-y)=\mu-\Sigma_{xy}\Sigma^{-1}_{yy}(A\mu+b-Ax-b)$

$$
\begin{align}
\therefore E(x \mid y) &= \mu_{x\cdot y} + \Sigma_{xy}\Sigma^{-1}_{yy}y\\\
&=\mu_x-\Sigma_{xy}\Sigma^{-1}_{yy}\mu_y+ \Sigma_{xy}\Sigma^{-1}_{yy}y \\\
&= \mu-\Sigma_{xy}\Sigma^{-1}_{yy}(\mu_y-y) \\\
&=\mu-\Sigma_{xy}\Sigma^{-1}_{yy}(A\mu+b-Ax-b)\\\
&=\mu-\Sigma_{xy}\Sigma^{-1}_{yy}A(\mu-x)\\\
&=\mu-\Lambda^{-1}A^T(A\Lambda^{-1}A^T+L^{-1})A(\mu-x)
\end{align}
$$

$$
\begin{align}
\therefore Var(x \mid y) & = \Sigma_{xx \cdot y}=\Sigma_{xx}-\Sigma_{xy}\Sigma^{-1}_{yy}\Sigma_{yx}=\Lambda^{-1}-\Lambda^{-1}A^T(A\Lambda^{-1}A^T+L^{-1})^{-1}A\Lambda^{ {-1}^{T} }
\end{align}
$$
